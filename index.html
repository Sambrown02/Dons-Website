What We Do

Set Smith

We carry out all aspects of modern set building using a vast array of materials, from the traditional set building techniques to incorporating modern speedline systems used in construction today. We Build from a single flattage to (at present) a 17thcentury village/settlement complete with stockade ! With over 120 years of experience in set construction experience between the 4 core team members, we can help you bring your designs and ideas to fruition quickly, calmly and to budget. The Core team experience include a Bristol Old Vic Master carpenter, a Royal Academy/National Theatre scenic artist, large scale project build and management including two exterior Hospital builds for BBC Casualty, large village set for New Worlds (company Productions), small sets, location builds and decorating. Being based at the Bottleyard we have a wellequipped workshop and are safety audited by C2 Safety for your peace of mind, we are a BBC supplier and are fully insured for large/small scale works. We have worked together for many years including my previous company. I have carried out builds all over the UK and In North America also. Credits in the last 2 years include, Bad Education the Movie, ABC televisions "Galavant & Galavant 2", BBC Tv "Our Girl", Trollied, Stella, New Worlds, BBC Casualty (Various Episodes), Deal or No deal and Mimic.
